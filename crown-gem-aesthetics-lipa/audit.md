## 1. Booking — your "Book Now" button sends patients to Facebook chat

**Finding:** On both Crown Gem Aesthetics websites (crowngemaesthetics.com and crowngemph.com), the "Book Now" button does not open a booking calendar. It redirects to the Crown Gem Aesthetics Facebook page. Patients have to message on Facebook and wait for a reply to set an appointment.

**Screenshot:** screenshot-gap-1.png

**Why this matters:**
- Patients searching at 9pm, on a Sunday, or during their lunch break cannot book. They message you, wait for a reply, and then decide. Most of them do not wait. They pick the next clinic.
- Every hour without a reply is a lost booking. If your team is busy or offline, that patient is gone.
- A live booking calendar lets patients pick their slot in under 2 minutes, any time of day.

**Competitor examples:**
- **Luxuriskin Aesthetic Clinic Lipa:** Active on Facebook (3,200+ followers on their Lipa branch page) and TikTok. Patients reach them across multiple platforms and get replies faster.
- **Aesthetic clinics on Setmore (free):** Patients pick a service, pick a time, get a confirmation right away. No staff time needed.

**Fix:** Add a FREE Setmore booking page. Link it to your Facebook "Book Now" button. Cost = ₱0. Setup = 30 minutes.

**Finding:** Neither crowngemaesthetics.com nor crowngemph.com has a live chat widget or an FAQ section. A patient who lands on the website and has a question about pricing, pain level, recovery time, or HMO coverage has no way to get an answer except to message on Facebook and wait.

**Screenshot:** none

**Why this matters:**
- Patients have questions before they book. If your website does not answer them, they either message and wait (and often do not follow through) or they leave and search somewhere else.
- Common questions that block bookings: "Is it painful?", "How long does it last?", "Do you accept HMO?", "What is the price?". Every unanswered question is a lost booking.

**Competitor examples:**
