## Direct local competitor — Luxuriskin Aesthetic Clinic Lipa

**Location:** Lipa City, Batangas (same city as you)
**Facebook:** https://www.facebook.com/LuxuriskinLipa/
**TikTok:** Active (handles patient content and treatment videos)
**Website:** None — Facebook-only presence
**Verdict:** Most established aesthetic clinic in Lipa online. Biggest local threat.

What they have that you don't:
- ✅ **3,200+ Facebook followers** on their Lipa branch page — built local trust and familiarity
- ✅ **Active TikTok** — posting treatment content, patient transformations, promos
- ✅ Consistent social media posting across platforms — patients see them regularly
- ✅ Clear local identity — "Luxuriskin Lipa" is recognizable in the Batangas market

What they DON'T have (your opening to leapfrog):
- ❌ No real website — all inquiries go through Facebook Messenger or TikTok comments
- ❌ No online booking calendar — still manual booking via social media
- ❌ No Google Maps listing confirmed for Lipa branch — invisible to "near me" Google searches
- ❌ No before/after gallery on a website — photos only on social feeds, easy to scroll past

**Net:** Luxuriskin has built the local audience. You have the brand credibility of a multi-branch network behind you. Neither of you has solved the digital infrastructure (booking calendar, GMB, website). The clinic that builds these first wins the Lipa market for the next 12 months.
