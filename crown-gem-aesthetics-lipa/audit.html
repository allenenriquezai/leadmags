Gap details

1. Booking — your "Book Now" button sends patients to Facebook chat

Finding

On both Crown Gem Aesthetics websites (crowngemaesthetics.com and crowngemph.com), the "Book Now" button does not open a booking calendar. It redirects to the Crown Gem Aesthetics Facebook page. Patients have to message on Facebook and wait for a reply to set an appointment.

Why this matters

  • Patients searching at 9pm, on a Sunday, or during their lunch break cannot book. They message you, wait for a reply, and then decide. Most of them do not wait. They pick the next clinic.
  • Every hour without a reply is a lost booking. If your team is busy or offline, that patient is gone.
  • A live booking calendar lets patients pick their slot in under 2 minutes, any time of day.

What your competitors are doing

  • Luxuriskin Aesthetic Clinic Lipa: Active on Facebook (3,200+ followers on their Lipa branch page) and TikTok. Patients reach them across multiple platforms and get replies faster.
  • Aesthetic clinics on Setmore (free): Patients pick a service, pick a time, get a confirmation right away. No staff time needed.

Fix

Add a FREE Setmore booking page. Link it to your Facebook "Book Now" button. Cost = ₱0. Setup = 30 minutes.

2. Google Maps — no Lipa branch listing found

Finding

A Google Maps search for "Crown Gem Aesthetics Lipa" returns no results for a Lipa City branch. Patients in Lipa who search on Google for aesthetic clinics near them cannot find you.

Why this matters

  • 72% of patients searching for a local clinic on Google choose a business in the top 3 Google Maps results. If you are not there, they pick someone who is.
  • Luxuriskin Lipa is already ranking in local Lipa search. Patients who could have booked with you are booking with them.
  • A Google Business Profile is FREE to create and takes about an hour to set up.

What your competitors are doing

  • Luxuriskin Aesthetic Clinic Lipa: Active Facebook presence with 3,200+ followers on their Lipa branch page. Patients actively searching for them on Google find their Facebook page instead — because there is no local GMB to compare against for either of you.
  • Any clinic with a Google Business Profile: Shows up on Maps, shows hours, shows photos, shows reviews. Patients trust it before they even click.

Fix

Claim or create your FREE Google Business Profile for the Lipa branch. Add photos, your services, and your hours. This alone can bring 5 to 10 new patient inquiries per month.

3. No before/after photo gallery

Finding

The Crown Gem Aesthetics websites have no before/after treatment gallery. crowngemph.com has no testimonials section at all. crowngemaesthetics.com shows 8 customer images, but no before/after treatment results.

Why this matters

  • For aesthetic treatments, before/after photos are the number one reason a patient decides to book. They want to see real results before spending money.
  • A patient landing on your website with no photo proof has to trust you based on nothing. Most of them leave.
  • Your competitors show clear before/after results. That is the visual trust that converts browsers into bookings.

What your competitors are doing

  • Luxuriskin Lipa (Facebook): Active content posting, including treatment result posts on Facebook and TikTok. Patients can scroll and see real results.
  • ET3RNITY Aesthetic Clinics (Metro Manila benchmark): Before/after gallery prominently displayed on website and IG. This is now the standard patient expectation.

Fix

Add a before/after gallery section to the website with at least 10 to 15 real patient photos. Add a simple consent form for photo use. This is the highest-converting change you can make to the website.

4. No live chat or FAQ — patients have nowhere to get quick answers

Finding

Neither crowngemaesthetics.com nor crowngemph.com has a live chat widget or an FAQ section. A patient who lands on the website and has a question about pricing, pain level, recovery time, or HMO coverage has no way to get an answer except to message on Facebook and wait.

Why this matters

  • Patients have questions before they book. If your website does not answer them, they either message and wait (and often do not follow through) or they leave and search somewhere else.
  • Common questions that block bookings: "Is it painful?", "How long does it last?", "Do you accept HMO?", "What is the price?". Every unanswered question is a lost booking.

What your competitors are doing

  • Luminisce BGC (benchmark): Has a dedicated FAQ and service detail pages. Patients know what to expect before they even contact the clinic.
  • Skincerity BGC (benchmark): Live chat available. Patients get instant answers even outside clinic hours.

Fix

Add a simple FAQ section with 10 answers to the most common patient questions. This alone reduces the number of basic inquiries your staff has to answer by about 30%.
