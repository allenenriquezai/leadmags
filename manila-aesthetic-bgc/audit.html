Gap details

1. Booking — "Book Now" goes to a contact form, not a calendar

Finding

The "Book Now" button on manilaaesthetic.com opens a contact page with your phone number and email address. There is no booking calendar. Patients who click "Book Now" still have to call or email, then wait for a reply to confirm their appointment.

Why this matters

  • BGC patients are busy professionals. They book things online. A patient who clicks "Book Now" and lands on a phone number will often close the tab and pick a clinic that lets them book in 2 minutes.
  • Luminisce BGC has a live booking page at luminisce.com and is also listed on Parlon, a popular PH beauty booking app. Patients who find Luminisce can confirm their appointment instantly. Patients who find you have to wait.
  • Every minute of delay between "I want to book" and "I have a confirmed slot" is a chance for that patient to change her mind.

What your competitors are doing

  • Luminisce BGC: Has its own booking page at luminisce.com and is on Parlon (book.parlon.ph). Patients confirm in under 2 minutes.
  • Skincerity BGC: Listed on third-party booking platforms. Available for same-day bookings online.
  • FREE option: Setmore lets you take online bookings at zero cost. Setup takes 30 minutes.

Fix

Add a real booking calendar. Setmore (FREE) or Calendly (FREE tier) lets patients pick a service and a slot without calling or emailing. Link it to the "Book Now" button on your website.

Why this matters

  • For aesthetic treatments, before/after photos are the number one reason a patient decides to book. Patients want to see real results before spending money on their face or skin.
  • A patient landing on your website sees a services list and a contact form. There is no visual proof that your treatments work. Most patients leave without contacting you.
  • Instagram (@manila_aesthetic_clinic_bgc) may have photos, but patients searching on Google land on your website first. If the website does not convert them, your Instagram presence does not matter.

What your competitors are doing

  • Luminisce BGC: Has a full photo gallery with treatment results. Patients can see exactly what to expect before booking.

Fix

Add a before/after gallery with at least 10 real patient photos with consent. Add 3 to 5 short testimonials. This is the highest-converting addition you can make to your website right now.
