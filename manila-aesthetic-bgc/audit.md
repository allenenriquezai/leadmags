## 1. Booking — "Book Now" goes to a contact form, not a calendar

**Finding:** The "Book Now" button on manilaaesthetic.com opens a contact page with your phone number and email address. There is no booking calendar. Patients who click "Book Now" still have to call or email, then wait for a reply to confirm their appointment.

**Screenshot:** screenshot-gap-1.png

**Why this matters:**
- BGC patients are busy professionals. They book things online. A patient who clicks "Book Now" and lands on a phone number will often close the tab and pick a clinic that lets them book in 2 minutes.
- Luminisce BGC has a live booking page at luminisce.com and is also listed on Parlon, a popular PH beauty booking app. Patients who find Luminisce can confirm their appointment instantly. Patients who find you have to wait.
- Every minute of delay between "I want to book" and "I have a confirmed slot" is a chance for that patient to change her mind.

**Competitor examples:**
- **Luminisce BGC:** Has its own booking page at luminisce.com and is on Parlon (book.parlon.ph). Patients confirm in under 2 minutes.
- **Skincerity BGC:** Listed on third-party booking platforms. Available for same-day bookings online.
- **FREE option:** Setmore lets you take online bookings at zero cost. Setup takes 30 minutes.

**Fix:** Add a real booking calendar. Setmore (FREE) or Calendly (FREE tier) lets patients pick a service and a slot without calling or emailing. Link it to the "Book Now" button on your website.
